Pool questions

A few common questions

Can you handle the landscaping around the pool too?

Yes. Planting, privacy, walls, lighting, and patio work can all be planned together.

What if our yard has slope or drainage issues?

That is common. Grade changes and drainage work are often part of making the yard work well.

Can the pool area match the rest of the property?

Yes. The pool area should feel like a natural extension of the home and landscape.
